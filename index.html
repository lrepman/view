Staying ProActive with the Coronavirus

I am a student, a mother, and a humanitarian. I sum it up with an air high five, because in these times of virtual hugs and air high-fives, we need to take the role of germ-prevention very seriously. I hope you find comfort and some humor in these times, keeping the power of a positive mind in sight. Here's to all of our health.
